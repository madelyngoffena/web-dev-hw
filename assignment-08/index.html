How to Draw an Eye

by Madelyn Goffena

The Three Steps to an Eye

  1. Step 1: Draw an almond shape.
  2. Step 2: Draw a circle were you want the eye to be looking then draw a smaller black circle in the center of the larger circle.
  3. Step 3:Erase the parts of the circles that are outside the almond shape then add details. You now have an eye! Good luck on drawing the other one!
